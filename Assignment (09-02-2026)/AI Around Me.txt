Google Maps Navigation – Uses ML to analyze traffic patterns, past data, and user movement to predict the fastest routes.

Voice Assistants (Alexa/Siri/Google Assistant) – ML processes speech recognition and natural language understanding to respond accurately to voice commands.

YouTube Recommendations – ML algorithms analyze watch history and preferences to suggest personalized videos.

Email Spam Filters – Machine learning identifies spam by analyzing patterns in email content and sender behavior.

Face Unlock in Smartphones – ML models recognize facial features and match them with stored biometric data.

Online Shopping Recommendations (Amazon/Flipkart) – ML studies browsing and purchase history to suggest relevant products.

Autocorrect & Predictive Text – ML predicts words based on typing patterns and commonly used language data.

Netflix/OTT Suggestions – Recommendation systems use ML to suggest movies or shows based on viewing habits.

Social Media Feed Personalization – ML prioritizes posts based on user interactions, likes, and interests.

Online Banking Fraud Detection – ML detects unusual transaction patterns to prevent fraudulent activities.
