#Spam Detection System Design

Features (Inputs):

	Presence of keywords (e.g., “free”, “win”, “offer”)
	Message length
	Number of links
	Sender information

Data Required:

	Collection of labeled messages (Spam / Not Spam)
	Historical email or SMS datasets

Possible Mistakes:

	False positives (important message marked as spam)
	False negatives (spam message not detected)
	Overfitting due to limited or biased data
